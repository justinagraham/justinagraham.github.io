Justin Graham

Technical cofounder. Architect. Systems engineer.

Building cross-platform native applications and game engines for over thirty years. Platform abstraction, graphics, audio, networking, simulation, parallel computing, scripting, content pipelines, build systems, and tooling. The systems layer. The parts that do the work.

Career

I started at LucasArts in 1990, where I cut my teeth porting the SCUMM engine to 32-bit protected mode and spent a decade there in systems and lead engineering roles. At Maxis, I built the audio engine for Spore and was lead audio engineer on The Sims 2 and SimCity 4. At Zynga, I led development on mobile titles and worked on web games at massive scale. I cofounded Elf Farm Games and built a cross-platform C++ engine from scratch , we shipped two kids games, one featured by Apple. I contributed to the Cocos2d-x engine at Chukong and cofounded SDKBox, a turn-key SDK integration toolchain for Unity, Unreal, and Cocos2d-x. I spent seven years at Unity in analytics, simulation infrastructure, sensor systems, and distributed rendering. I hold a patent for that work. At Gallium Studios, I built backend infrastructure and AI systems for Proxi , avatars instilled with player memories and brought to life through generative AI.

Present

Today I'm building NJiN, a cross-platform modern game engine. I cofounded AdFab, a playable ad company built on NJiN technology. Thirty years of experience has culminated here, a compact performant game engine that runs everywhere.
